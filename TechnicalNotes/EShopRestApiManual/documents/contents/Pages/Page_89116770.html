7. Appendix

7.1. CA Certificate

This section describes the CA certificates to use when authenticating a server certificate.

Note:

On 3DS and Wii U, the CA certificates are installed on the system.

Root Certificate

Use NintendoCA-G3.pem or NintendoCA-G3.der to authenticate the Nintendo eShop server certificate.

Intermediate CA Certificate

Use NintendoClass2CA-G3.pem or NintendoClass2CA-G3.der to authenticate an independent server certificate.
