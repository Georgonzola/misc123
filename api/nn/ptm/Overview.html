nn::ptm Namespace

Description

The namespace of the power supply and clock library.

Namespaces

nn::ptm::CTR Namespace for the CTR power supply and clock library. (It can also be written without the CTR portion.)

Classes

nn::ptm::ResultOverWriteAlarm Indicates that the alarm has already been configured.
nn::ptm::ResultNoAlarm Indicates that the alarm has not been configured.
nn::ptm::ResultNotRegistered Indicates that an alarm notification event has not been set.
nn::ptm::ResultWrongArgument Indicates that an argument is wrong.
nn::ptm::ResultInvalidAlarm Indicates that the date specified for the alarm is out of range.
