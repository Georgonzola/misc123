nn::ulcd::CTR::DynamicStereoCamera::OccupancyVolume Structure

Header File: nn/ulcd.h

Syntax

struct OccupancyVolume
{
   math::VEC3 CameraCenter;
   math::VEC3 ForwardDirection;
   math::VEC3 OrthoUpDirection;
   math::VEC3 RightDirection;
   f32 NearPlaneDepth;
   f32 NearLeft;
   f32 NearRight;
   f32 NearBottom;
   f32 NearTop;
   f32 CameraLeft;
   f32 CameraRight;
   f32 CameraBottom;
   f32 CameraTop;
};

Description

The OccupancyVolume structure represents the space that the camera occupies. This structure can be obtained from the output of the nn::ulcd::CTR::DynamicStereoCamera::GetOccupancyVolume function. It provides information about the range of possible movement by the camera when using the DynamicPerspective feature. It can be used for things such as camera and object hit detection.

Member Variables

CameraCenter math::VEC3 The center coordinates of the camera. These coordinates are on the camera plane.
ForwardDirection math::VEC3 The front-facing vector from the camera to the near clipping plane.
OrthoUpDirection math::VEC3 The camera up vector. Orthogonal to the forward-facing vector.
RightDirection math::VEC3 The camera right vector. Calculated as the cross product of the front-facing vector and the up vector.
NearPlaneDepth f32 Distance from the camera to the near clipping plane.
NearLeft f32 The left edge of the camera occupancy space on the near clipping plane.
NearRight f32 The right edge of the camera occupancy space on the near clipping plane.
NearBottom f32 The bottom edge of the camera occupancy space on the near clipping plane.
NearTop f32 The top edge of the camera occupancy space on the near clipping plane.
CameraLeft f32 The left edge of the camera occupancy space on the camera plane.
CameraRight f32 The right edge of the camera occupancy space on the camera plane.
CameraBottom f32 The bottom edge of the camera occupancy space on the camera plane.
CameraTop f32 The top edge of the camera occupancy space on the camera plane.
